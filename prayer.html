Prayer

Jesus, thank You for carrying me through every season of growth. Thank You for sustaining me during moments when understanding felt small and faith felt fragile. Thank You for every lesson, correction, challenge, and invitation into deeper places.

Remove every desire within me to remain comfortable where growth has stopped. Teach me obedience even during moments when understanding feels incomplete. Strengthen discernment within me. Build endurance within me. Form maturity within my heart through every circumstance and every season.

Help me trust Your voice when explanations feel absent. Help me remain faithful when silence feels long. Help me choose obedience rather than comfort and transformation rather than familiarity.

Allow my life to become useful for Your kingdom. Allow strength formed through trials to encourage others. Continue leading me beyond shallow places and into everything You desire.

In the name of Jesus, Amen.

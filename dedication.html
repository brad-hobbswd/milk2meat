Dedication

This book is dedicated to every person who has felt a stirring deep within their spirit and quietly wondered whether more exists beyond routine faith.

Dedicated to those who have sensed holy hunger, those who have grown weary of repetition, and those who know Jesus continues calling people into deeper places.

Dedicated to believers who have walked through correction, stretching seasons, silence, and uncomfortable growth while continuing to follow Jesus anyway.

Jesus still leads people deeper. Jesus still transforms willing hearts. Jesus still calls believers from milk toward meat.

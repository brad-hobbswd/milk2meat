Introduction

Spiritual growth begins with a relationship with Jesus, yet growth was never meant to stop at the beginning. Many believers learn foundational truths and experience the joy of new faith while remaining in places where familiarity becomes comfort.

From Milk to Meat explores the journey beyond spiritual infancy and into maturity. Through Scripture, personal experiences, and seasons of testing, this book invites readers into a deeper walk with Jesus where faith becomes strengthened through obedience, endurance, and transformation.

Growth rarely arrives through comfort alone. Pressure, correction, silence, stretching seasons, and unexpected moments often become places where Jesus forms deeper maturity within us. Those moments may feel difficult, yet they often become turning points where faith begins moving beyond routine into lasting transformation.

This journey is an invitation. Jesus continues calling people deeper. Jesus continues leading hearts from milk toward meat.

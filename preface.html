Preface

Growth rarely happens where comfort remains untouched. Familiar places often feel safe, predictable, and easy to remain within. Yet Jesus continually calls people deeper. Spiritual maturity does not happen accidentally. Growth develops through surrender, obedience, correction, stretching seasons, and learning to trust God beyond familiar places.

Many lessons found throughout this book were formed during personal moments of prayer, dreams, challenges, quiet seasons, and encounters with truth that pressed beyond surface understanding. Certain moments felt uncomfortable. Others felt confusing. Every season carried purpose.

Milk serves a valuable purpose in spiritual life. Foundations matter. Beginnings matter. Early growth matters. Yet remaining where growth once began eventually becomes a place of limitation rather than nourishment.

This journey carries a simple invitation. Continue growing. Continue following Jesus. Continue responding when He calls you deeper.
